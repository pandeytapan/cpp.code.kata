Surface #2

To reach surface # 2 you have to return the sum of the total number of vowels in each substring. "abba" will have substrings "a, ab, bb, ba, abb, bba, abba", and the number of vowels in each substring will be 1 1, 0, 1, 1, 1, 2 and the total number will be sum of all vowels presence e.g. ðŸ‘‡
